About RIP My Idea

RIP My Idea is a digital graveyard for the startups, side projects, apps, products, and dreams that never made it — not because they were bad ideas, but because building things is hard. Money runs out. Passion fades. Co-founders fight. Life gets in the way. None of that makes your idea less worthy of a proper farewell.

This is a place for closure. You tried something. You deserve to mark it. Write the epitaph, lay it to rest, and move forward knowing it happened and it mattered.

Please note that all user-submitted content is subject to moderation. Any content determined to be harmful, defamatory, misleading, abusive, or in violation of applicable laws or community standards may be removed by our content moderation team at their sole discretion and without prior notice.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this for?

Anyone who has ever started something and couldn't see it through — founders, developers, designers, writers, students, and dreamers of all kinds. If you had an idea and let it go, this graveyard has a plot with your name on it.

Is my submission visible to others?

Right now, gravestones are stored locally in your browser — only you can see them on your device. A shared public graveyard is coming in the next version, where every buried idea will be visible to the world. Sign up for the waitlist to be notified.

Can I submit someone else's idea?

Please only submit ideas, projects, or businesses that you personally worked on or were directly involved with. Submitting content about others without their knowledge or consent is not permitted and may be removed by our moderation team.

What happens to content that violates the rules?

User-submitted content that is found to be harmful, false, defamatory, abusive, or otherwise inappropriate will be removed. We reserve the right to remove any submission at our discretion in order to maintain the integrity and respectful tone of the platform.

Why are only letters allowed in the name field?

The name field is for the person's real name or a recognisable alias. We restrict it to letters to keep the graveyard respectful and to prevent random characters or numbers from appearing where a person's name should be.

Can I resurrect my idea?

No. Once buried, an idea rests in peace. This is a graveyard, not a hospital. That said — if someone else picks it up and builds it, that's a story worth telling.

What is the waitlist for?

The next version of RIP My Idea will launch a real shared public graveyard — where every buried idea is visible to the world. Leave your email and you will be among the first to know when it goes live.

Rest in pieces, every last one of them.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how RIP My Idea collects, uses, and handles information provided by users of this platform. By accessing or using this site, you acknowledge and agree to the terms described below.

Data Storage

All gravestone data submitted through this platform is currently stored locally in your browser via localStorage. No personal information is transmitted to or retained on any external server at this time. Your data remains entirely on your own device.

Information You Provide

When submitting a gravestone, you may optionally provide your name, website URL, and social media profile link. This information is stored locally and, in future versions of the platform, may be displayed publicly as part of your gravestone listing. Providing this information is entirely voluntary.

Email Address and Waitlist

If you choose to join the waitlist, your email address will be collected and stored securely for the sole purpose of notifying you when the public graveyard launches. We will not share, sell, or otherwise disclose your email address to any third party, nor will we use it for any purpose beyond the scope of this notification without your explicit prior consent.

User-Generated Content and Moderation

Content submitted by users — including gravestone names, epitaphs, and associated details — is intended for public display in future versions of this platform. Users are advised not to include sensitive personal information in their submissions. RIP My Idea reserves the right to review, edit, or permanently remove any user-generated content that is found to be harmful, defamatory, misleading, abusive, unlawful, or otherwise in violation of applicable laws or the community standards of this platform. Such removal may occur without prior notice to the submitting user.

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party analytics services. The only browser storage mechanism used by this platform is localStorage, which stores gravestone data locally on your device.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically as the platform evolves. Continued use of the site following the publication of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. The most current version of this policy will always be available on this page.
